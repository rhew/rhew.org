Attack Defense

1. Grab wrist with one hand

Make fist, pull hand free, back fist to jaw

4. Grab waist from behind (inside arms)

Stomp foot, knee behind leg, elbow to solar plexus

5. Grab waist from behind (outside arms)

Head butt, reach down, pull up ankle, side kick to solar plexus/jaw

10. Bear hug from front

Jab thumbs in ribs, hip throw
