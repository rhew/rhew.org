Day 2/3: London

See Big Ben, Parliament, Westminster Abbey, Piccadilly Circus, St. Paul's, and more, ending at Buckingham Palace for the Changing of the Guard (if scheduled).

Day 2/3: Stonehenge, Salisbury, Bath

View prehistoric ruins at Stonehenge on the way to Salisbury to visit Salisbury Cathedral. Proceed to Bath for a guided tour of the ancient Roman Baths. Return to London for dinner.
