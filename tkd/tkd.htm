Ten Articles of Student Commitment
  • Be loyal to your country.
  • Honor your parents.
  • Be loving between husband and wife.
  • Be cooperative between brother and sister.
  • Be faithful to your friends.
  • Be respectful to your elders.
  • Establish trust between teacher and student.
  • Use good judgement before killing living things.
  • Never retreat in battle.
  • Always finish what you start.

Tae Kwon Do Belts
Belt Meaning

The White Belt symbolizes purity, lack of knowledge, and humility.

The Yellow Belt symbolizes the color of sunlight early in the morning, therefore a new beginning of knowledge.

The Green Belt sybbolizes the color of spring and new growth.

Orange

The Blue Belt symbolizes the color of the sky, reaching high, youth, and ambition.

High Blue

Brown

Purple

The Red Belt symbolizes the color of blood or the sun, developing a strong foundation for your body and nature. Red is also a warning of danger.

A Black Belt contains all colors, signifying the deepest and most dense knowledge, the accumulation from every preceding level.
